# Merchant Admin Portal Guide
*Namibia Computer Technology Investment CC — Main Administrator Manual*

Welcome to the **Merchant Admin Portal** (`admin.html`). This dashboard is designed to provide you with complete control over your e-commerce platform. It runs on a reactive local database (`StoreDb`) which automatically syncs edits across the storefront, cart, checkout, and admin pages in real-time.

## 1. Accessing the Portal
You can access the Administrator Dashboard in two ways:
* **Footer Navigation:** Click on the **"Merchant Admin Portal"** link located under the "Company & Trust" column of the footer on any page.
* **Direct URL:** Navigate directly to `http://localhost:3000/admin.html` in your web browser.

## 2. Tab 1: Overview Analytics
This is your main dashboard workspace for business health metrics. It contains:
* **Metric Cards:**
  * **Gross Revenue:** Total order value placed on the store in Namibian Dollars (N$).
  * **Orders Received:** Count of all submitted orders in the database.
  * **Average Order Value (AOV):** Calculated as `Gross Revenue / Orders Received`.
  * **AdSense CPC Revenue:** Total simulated revenue generated by customer clicks on AdSense banners.
* **Sales Trends Graph:** A Chart.js line graph displaying your daily gross sales revenue and AdSense earnings trends over the last 6 days.

## 3. Tab 2: Product Database CRUD (Add, Edit, Delete, Price Change)
The **Product Database CRUD** (Create, Read, Update, Delete) tab is where you manage your listings.

### A. How to Add a New Product
1. Click the blue **"+ Add Product"** button in the top right corner of the product panel.
2. In the modal form, fill out the following fields:
   * **Product Name:** Enter the title (e.g. *HP EliteBook 840 G8*).
   * **Category:** Choose from *Laptops*, *Desktops & Towers*, *PC Components*, or *Accessories*.
   * **Condition:** Select *New*, *Open Box*, or *Refurbished/Used*.
   * **Price (N$):** Input the catalog retail price.
   * **Stock Quantity:** Input the initial number of units available (e.g. *1*).
   * **Brand:** Specify the manufacturer brand (e.g. *HP*).
   * **Mark Featured:** Check this if you want the product to appear in the "Featured Products" grid on the Homepage.
   * **Description Summary:** Write a brief marketing summary and specs outline.
3. Click **"Save Changes"**.
   * *System Logic:* The database automatically creates a unique ID (e.g., `lap-47`), binds a default image asset based on the category (e.g. `assets/hero_laptop.png`), and populates the specifications sheet with the Brand, Warranty details, and Condition.

### B. How to Change a Price or Edit a Product
1. Locate the product in the table.
2. Click the yellow **Edit icon** (Pencil icon) on the right side of the product row.
3. The edit form modal will appear, preloaded with the product's current values.
4. Modify the **Price (N$)**, brand details, description, or stock.
5. Click **"Save Changes"** to save. The price update is instantly pushed to the shop page.

### C. How to Delete a Product
1. Locate the product row in the table.
2. Click the red **Trash icon** on the right side of the row.
3. Confirm the pop-up warning, and the item will be permanently removed from the catalog.

## 4. Tab 3: Inventory Stock Control & Alert System
The **Inventory Control Panel** allows you to monitor and replenish stock values at a glance.

* **Status Alert Badges:**
  * **Out of Stock (Red):** Displayed when the stock count is exactly `0`.
  * **Low Stock (Yellow):** Displayed when the stock count is between `1` and `5` units (ideal for tracking fast-selling refurbished stock).
  * **In Stock (Green/Standard):** Displayed when stock is `6` or more.
* **Quick Restock Action:** To increase stock count quickly without launching the edit modal:
  1. Locate the product row.
  2. Click the **"Restock"** button.
  3. A prompt will ask: *"Enter Restock Quantity"*.
  4. Type in the amount (e.g., *5*) and click OK. The stock count will immediately increment in the table and live store databases.

## 5. Tab 4: Orders & Fulfillment Management
The **Order Fulfillment Logs** show all customer transactions, shipping destinations, payment methods, and fulfillment statuses.

* **Fulfillment Statuses:**
  * **Pending:** Newly placed order (default status).
  * **Processing:** Payment checked / order packed.
  * **Completed:** Shipped or handed over to the customer.
* **Fulfilling Bank Transfer (EFT) Orders:**
  1. When an order is placed via Bank Transfer, verify that you received the payment in your **FNB/RMB Account (64280109206)**.
  2. Match the **Order Ref ID** from the customer's proof of payment.
  3. Locate the matching order reference in the Orders Table.
  4. Click the action button (e.g., **"Process"** or **"Deliver"**) to cycle the status badge to **Completed**.

## 6. Tab 5: Promo Discount Coupons Creator
Manage active coupon codes applied by customers in the checkout summary.

### A. Creating a Coupon
1. Enter a **Coupon Code** (use uppercase characters, e.g. *NAMTECH20*).
2. Choose **Discount Type**:
   * **Percentage (%):** Deducts a percentage from the cart subtotal (e.g. *15* for 15% off).
   * **Fixed Amount (N$):** Deducts a flat cash value from the cart subtotal (e.g. *50* for N$50 off).
3. Set **Minimum Cart Subtotal:** (e.g., *1500* for coupon eligibility on orders over N$1,500).
4. **Free Shipping Toggle:** Tick the check-box if you want this coupon to set delivery fees to N$0.00.
5. Click **"Save Coupon"** to register it in active store coupons.

## 7. Tab 6: Google AdSense Simulator
Your platform includes an AdSense simulator. This section is used to manage ad zones and analyze monetization metrics.

* **Monetization Dashboard:**
  * **Impressions:** Displays the count of times ad banners were loaded.
  * **Clicks:** Displays total clicks simulated by the user.
  * **CTR (Click-Through Rate):** Displays `Clicks / Impressions` ratio.
  * **CPC Income:** Earned income from ad click simulations.
* **Configurations:**
  * **Enable AdSense Globally Toggle:** Unchecking this box removes all AdSense display placeholders from every page on the store.
  * **Active Ad Zones Table:** View and toggle individual page placements (e.g. index hero banner, shop sidebar, contact aside banner) on or off.

## 8. Automatic Client-Storefront Syncing
The portal's database is backed by reactive `localStorage` triggers. 
* Whenever you add, edit, restock, or delete products, or modify active coupons, a **custom update event** is fired inside the browser:
  `window.dispatchEvent(new CustomEvent('nct_db_update', ...))`
* This means that you do **not** need to manually redeploy or compile your codebase. Any edits you make in the Admin Portal are instantly applied to the live catalog shown to customers visiting your site.
