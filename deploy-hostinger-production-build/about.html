We believe that every PC user deserves exceptional hardware, whether they are configuring an elite liquid-cooled gaming workstation or seeking cost-effective certified refurbished equipment.

By cleaning, repasting, and guaranteeing our used/refurbished stock, we help reduce electronic waste while keeping premium computing accessible across our locations in Otjiwarongo and Rundu, Namibia.

GDPR Privacy & Cookie Policy

NamCompTech is committed to complete transparency regarding client data processing. In compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and regional privacy mandates, this section details what information we store, how cookies support Google AdSense simulations, and how you can manage your preferences.

1. Use of Cookies

We utilize local memory triggers (cookies and browser localStorage) to manage your shopping cart items, bookmarked wishlists, system comparison panels, and active dark/light styles. These operational tokens contain no personal information.

2. Simulated Google AdSense Integration

We host non-intrusive AdSense zones to fund custom rig research. If you grant consent (Accept Cookies), we display **personalized advertisements** correlating to your category views (e.g. showing laptop deals if you searched for notebooks). If you decline consent, we serve generic, static, privacy-safe text announcements. No browser tracking histories are compiled.

3. User Rights under GDPR

  • Right to Erasure (Forget Me): Clear your browser storage to wipe profile modifications and orders history instantly.
  • Right of Access: Review your stored cart, profile, and order records transparently in our customer portal.
  • Right to Rectification: Edit your name, email, and shipping destinations dynamically in the user portal.
